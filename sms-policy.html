SMS Communications Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Leverage AI LLC ("we," "us," or "our") provides AI-powered SMS communication services on behalf of our business clients in the home service and trade industry. This policy describes how SMS messages are sent, how recipients can opt in and opt out, and how phone numbers are handled.

Consent & Opt-In

End users opt in to receive SMS messages by providing their phone number at the time of service booking with one of our business clients. By providing a phone number during the booking process, the end user consents to receive SMS communications related to their service request.

Types of Messages

We may send the following types of SMS messages on behalf of our business clients:

  • Customer support responses and service inquiries
  • Escalation notifications to business owners
  • AI-assisted conversation replies

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the nature and status of your service request. You will only receive messages that are relevant to your interaction with the business.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. Once you opt out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent unless you re-subscribe.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message. You may also contact us at the email address listed on our website.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Privacy

We do not sell, rent, or share phone numbers with third parties for marketing purposes. Phone numbers are used solely for the purpose of delivering service-related communications on behalf of our business clients. For more information, see our Privacy Policy.
